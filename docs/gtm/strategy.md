# TIPZ Go-to-Market Strategy

Comprehensive GTM plan for the TIPZ private micro-tipping platform.

---

## Executive Summary

TIPZ launches as the first private micro-tipping solution for crypto-native creators. Our GTM strategy focuses on organic growth through the Zcash community, targeted KOL relationships, and viral product mechanics.

**Launch Timeline**: 1 week sprint to MVP launch
**Primary Channel**: X (Twitter)
**Primary Audience**: Crypto creators with privacy awareness
**Growth Model**: Creator-led viral adoption

## Target Segments

### Primary: Crypto-Native Creators

**Profile**:
- Active on X/Twitter
- Already accepts crypto tips or donations
- Privacy-conscious (uses VPNs, concerned about doxxing)
- 5K-500K followers (sweet spot for personal engagement)

**Examples**:
- DeFi educators and analysts
- Privacy advocates and researchers
- Independent crypto journalists
- NFT artists
- Open-source developers

**Why they care**:
- Don't want income publicly visible
- Already understand crypto UX
- Low friction to adopt

### Secondary: Privacy-First Creators

**Profile**:
- May not be crypto-native yet
- Strong privacy beliefs
- Often political/controversial content
- Concerned about platform censorship

**Why they care**:
- Financial privacy paramount
- Decentralized = uncensorable
- Self-custody matters

### Tertiary: Zcash Community

**Profile**:
- Already holds and uses ZEC
- Active in forums/Discord
- Technical understanding
- Community-oriented

**Why they care**:
- Believe in shielded by default
- Want to see Zcash adoption
- Will evangelize authentic Zcash apps
