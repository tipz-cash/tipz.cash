# Seed Creator Target List

> Track potential early adopters for TIPZ launch.
> Goal: 10+ registered creators before grant submission.

---

## Ideal Seed Creator Profile

**Must have:**
- Active on X (posts at least weekly)
- Has a Zcash shielded address OR willing to set one up
- Willing to post verification tweet publicly

**Nice to have:**
- Already knows/uses Zcash
- 1K+ followers (but smaller engaged audiences work too)
- Creates content related to privacy, crypto, or digital rights
- Open to providing feedback

---

## How to Find Creators

### Search Strategies

1. **X Bio Search:** Search for "Zcash" or "ZEC" in bios
2. **Follower Mining:** Look at who follows @ZcashFoundation, @ElectricCoinCo, @zaboris
3. **Forum Contributors:** Active users on forum.zcashcommunity.com
4. **Reddit:** r/zec contributors
5. **Privacy Communities:** Follow chains from privacy advocates

### Quick Research Script

Before DMing, check:
- [ ] Are their DMs open?
- [ ] Do they engage with followers?
- [ ] Have they mentioned tipping/support before?
- [ ] What content do they create?

### If They Say "What's Zcash?"

```
Great question! Zcash is like Bitcoin but with built-in privacy.

When you receive a tip to a "shielded" address, no one can see:
- How much you received
- Who sent it
- Your total balance

It's financial privacy by default.

Zashi is the easiest wallet to set up:
https://electriccoin.co/zashi/

Takes about 2 minutes. Want me to walk you through it?
```

### If They're Interested But No Zcash Address

```
Awesome! Setting up a Zcash shielded address takes ~2 min.

1. Download Zashi wallet: https://electriccoin.co/zashi/
2. Create new wallet (save your seed phrase!)
3. In Zashi, copy your "Unified Address" (starts with 'u1')
   OR tap "Show Shielded Address" to get one starting with 'zs1'
4. Paste that address in TIPZ registration: tipz.cash/register

Let me know if you hit any snags!
```
